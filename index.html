About Me

I am a second-year master student at UM-SJTU Joint Institute, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Yibo Pi.

My research interests lie in networking systems and wireless communication, with a particular focus on 5G systems.

In 2023, I received my B.S. degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications.
